# tsbi5


The first screen (Options) in tsbi5 contains 5-6 tabs:


*   [*Options* ](13_02_tsbi5_options.md)- simulation parameters.
*   [*Moisture*](../24Miscellaneous/24_60_tsbi5_moisture.md) (*only available if the module for simulation of moisture transport in constructions is present*) - choice of constructions and start conditions.
*   [*Simulation* ](13_04_tsbi5_simulation.md)- carrying out a simulation.
*   [*HeatBalance* ](13_07_tsbi5_HeatBalance.md)- a summary (table and graph) of the calculated heat balances for the building and the individual thermal zones.
*   [*Parameters* ](13_08_tsbi5_Parameters.md)- option of compiling lists of parameters for detailed analysis.
*   [*Tables* ](13_09_tsbi5_Tables.md)- a summary (table and graph) of the calculated values (hourly values) for the parameters selected in *Parameters*.
