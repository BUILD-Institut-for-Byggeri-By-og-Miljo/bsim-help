# tsbi5


The first screen (Options) in tsbi5 contains 5-6 tabs:


*   [*Options* ](https://help.bsim.dk/support/kb/articles/nmDBKR9y/tsbi5---options)- simulation parameters.
*   [Moisture](https://help.bsim.dk/support/kb/articles/XQYdbPmP/tsbi5---fugt) (*only available if the module for simulation of moisture transport in constructions is present*) - choice of constructions and start conditions.
*   [*Simulation* ](https://help.bsim.dk/support/kb/articles/DQ2xjyWV/tsbi5---simulation)- carrying out a simulation.
*   [*HeatBalance* ](https://help.bsim.dk/support/kb/articles/wmjn57mV/tsbi5---heatbalance)- a summary (table and graph) of the calculated heat balances for the building and the individual thermal zones.
*   [*Parameters* ](https://help.bsim.dk/support/kb/articles/nmDBAR9y/tsbi5---parameters)- option of compiling lists of parameters for detailed analysis.
*   [*Tables* ](https://help.bsim.dk/support/kb/articles/BWzdLlQE/tsbi5---tables)- a summary (table and graph) of the calculated values (hourly values) for the parameters selected in *Parameters*.
