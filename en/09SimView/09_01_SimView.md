# SimView
*SimView* contains a simple editor for creating and editing data models. To get the best out of the model editor, it is necessary to understand how it works. The individual programs are designed to handle a convex three-dimensional geometry. In other words, from any point in the space it must be possible to "see" all the other surfaces in the space to obtain a correct calculation. If, for example, an L-shaped [thermal zone](https://bsim.outseta.com/support/kb/articles/rm0x8ZmX/thermal-zone-property) is to be created, it is easiest to construct it in the form of two rectangular spaces with an opening that fills the entire interface between them.
