# SimView - Adding spaces to thermal zones

One or more spaces can be specified as being in the same thermal zone. A space can be attached to a thermal zone by holding the left mouse button down on the desired space while dragging it to the desired thermal zone and dropping it on the name. Several spaces can be attached in the same way.

A space can be removed from a thermal zone in a similar way by dragging it out of the thermal zone and dropping it on the building or in another thermal zone. When a space is removed from a thermal zone, all information on the systems attached to the thermal zone is removed at the same time.

Be aware that heating systems with a final output installed could have trouble maintaining the desired room temperature if more spaces are attached, resulting in a greater heat loss for the thermal zone.
