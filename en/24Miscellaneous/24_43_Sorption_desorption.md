Absorption/Desorption

*   Input values of relative humidity (-) and moisture content (kg/kg) for points belonging together at the absorption (desorption) curve for the material. First point is always assumed to be (0, 0), and can be left out. The values are inputted in order by increasing relative humidity.

DeltaRH

*   Input value(s) for the material curve in the hygroscopeic area, as values of relative humidity and (-) and moisture permeability (kg/m s Pa) belonging together.

DeltaU
