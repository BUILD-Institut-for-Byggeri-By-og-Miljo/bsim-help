# Building Property

*BUILDING* informs about the gross and net volume of the current building.

*   *Hide View* offers the possibility to hide the geometric drawing of the actual building - especially useful in large models with more buildings.

*   *Rotation* is the rotation of the model. A positive value will rotate a North facing face towards East.

*   *Height:* Is the total height of the building. This number is used when simulating natural ventilation to calculate the correct wind pressure if only a part of the building have been modeled.

*   *Current Building* tells BSim which building (if more than one exists in the model) to simulate in XSun and tsbi5.

*   *Site* informs about the current site (the geographic location and climate) of the model.

*   Information on the *design heat loss* of the current building is found under the *Site-caption*. This value is calculated from the U-values of the constructions and the design temperatures given at the [*Heatloss* ](../09SimView/09_11_Model_information.md)tab from the [*Defaults* ](../10Thermal_zones/10_06_SimView_Default_constructions.md)menu entry in the *[SimView-menu](../06BSim_Program_structure/06_06_SimView_Menu.md).* The design heat loss will **only** be shown if the design temperatures on the *Heatloss* tab have been accepted by clicking OK.
