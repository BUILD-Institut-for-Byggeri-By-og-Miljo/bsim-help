# Join the debate and information forum 
Questions on BSim can be asked on the Danish Building Research Institute e-mail debate and information forum. The debate forum is a closed list, which means that you must be a subscriber to receive e-mail from the list and to be able to submit questions to the list. Two BSim lists exists at Danish Building Research Institute, one primarily in Danish, one in English and one in German.

To join the English list, you can send an e-mail to maiser@SBi.dk with the body text SUBSCRIBE BSIM-UK, or you can fill in a form at the Danish Building Research Institute's Internet page: [Discussion and information forum for BSim](https://www.build.aau.dk/til-byggebranchen).

To join the German list, you can send an e-mail to maiser@SBi.dk with the body text SUBSCRIBE BSIM-DE, or you can fill in a form at the Danish Building Research Institute's Internet page: [Discussion and information forum for BSim](https://www.build.aau.dk/til-byggebranchen).

You can join your e-mail address to the Danish list by sending an e-mail to maiser@SBi.dk with the body text SUBSCRIBE BSIM, or via Danish Building Research Institute's Internet page: [Discussion and information forum for BSim](https://www.build.aau.dk/til-byggebranchen).

An additional advantage of joining the debate forum on BSim is that all information about new versions and courses will be distributed here.

Pay attention to the fact that **all** messages to the list will be distributed automatically to **all** members of the list - and no advertising or spam mail will be accepted.

Based on the questions on the list, Danish Building Research Institute will maintain a FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions and answers to these) on the Internet. The latest version of the FAQ is available from [Danish Building Research Institute's Internet pages.](https://www.build.aau.dk/til-byggebranchen)
